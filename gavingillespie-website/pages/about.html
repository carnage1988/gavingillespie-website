About Me

The best engineers never stop asking why.

I’ve built my career around understanding how technology works, improving systems where I can, and helping others get the best from them.

My Story

I didn’t get into technology because I wanted to work in IT. I got into it because I hated not understanding how things worked.

Growing up, my dad never let me throw broken technology away without first trying to understand why it had failed. That curiosity has stayed with me.

My Journey

I started on busy service desks supporting healthcare and managed service environments, where I learned communication, documentation and troubleshooting under pressure.

Today I work as a Technical Consultant supporting enterprise applications, Microsoft technologies, Linux environments and migration projects.

“Once I get the itch to know something, I have to research it, understand it and be able to talk about it.”

How I Like To Work

When I don’t understand something, I don’t leave it there. I research it, break it, fix it, build it again and then try to explain it clearly.

Where I’m Heading

My goal isn’t to know everything. It’s to keep learning. I’m focusing on infrastructure engineering, automation, cloud technologies and DevOps.
